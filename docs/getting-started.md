# Getting started

## Creating an account

1. Visit the registry and click **Sign in** on the landing page.
2. On the sign-in page, choose **Continue with Google** or enter your email
   address to sign up with a password.
3. Complete the sign-up flow. Your account is created immediately, but starts
   in **pending** status.

## Account approval

All new accounts require admin approval before you can access the registry.

After signing in for the first time you will see:

> **Your account is pending approval.**
> An admin will review your request.

This is the `/pending` page. You cannot browse the registry until your account
is approved.

Once an admin approves your account you can sign in normally and access the
registry. If your account is rejected you will see a `/rejected` page and will
need to contact the registry administrator.

## Account statuses

| Status | What you see after sign-in |
|---|---|
| `pending` | Waiting for approval page — no registry access |
| `active` | Full access based on your role |
| `rejected` | Account not approved page — no registry access |

## Roles

Every active account has one of three roles. Your role is assigned by an
administrator:

| Role | Who it's for |
|---|---|
| `user` | Standard member — browse the registry and submit dogs |
| `kennel_owner` | Owner of a registered kennel — manage dogs under your kennel |
| `admin` | Registry administrator — full access |

See [Role reference](roles.md) for a full breakdown of what each role can do.

## Signing out

Use the account button in the top-right corner of any page to sign out.
On the pending/rejected pages, a **Sign out** button is shown directly.
